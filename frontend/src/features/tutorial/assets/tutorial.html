Walk around with WASD or arrow keys
Look around with mouse
Click or use [Space] to gather resources or attack
Gather some wood
Use the craft menu in the top left corner to make a wood club from 10 wood.
Now equip your club by clicking it in your inventory.
It's pretty cold. Craft a campfire out of 30 wood and 5 stone.
Now select the campfire and place it on the ground using click or [Space].
One last thing: Gather some berries and click them to eat, so you don't starve to death.
That's it. Get out there and try to survive as long as possible!
